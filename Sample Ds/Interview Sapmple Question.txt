AI Engineer Interview Questions

--- Technical Questions (12) ---

1. What is the difference between verification and validation in software testing?
2. Can you explain the different types of testing (unit, integration, system, regression, etc.) and when you would use each?
3. How do you decide which test cases to automate?
4. Write a sample test case for the login page of a web application.
5. Describe a time when you found a critical bug. How did you document and communicate it?
6. What is black-box testing? Provide an example.
7. How do you perform API testing? Which tools do you use?
8. What is the difference between severity and priority? Give examples.
9. What experience do you have with test automation frameworks? (e.g., Selenium, Cypress, Appium)
10. How would you handle a situation where developers dispute a reported defect?
11. What are your strategies for testing mobile applications across different devices and platforms?
12. What is a Test Plan? What are the key components?

--- HR/Non-Technical Questions (10) ---

1. Tell us about yourself and your background in Quality Assurance.
2. Why do you want to work with our company?
3. What do you know about our products or services?
4. How do you handle tight deadlines and pressure?
5. How do you deal with receiving negative feedback from a manager or peer?
6. Describe a time you resolved a conflict in your team.
7. How do you keep yourself updated with industry trends and new technologies?
8. What does teamwork mean to you, and how do you contribute to a positive team environment?
9. Where do you see your career in three years?
10. Do you have any questions for us?
